About BETO

BETO is a Cameroonian interior decoration company specializing in the use of staff — a noble plaster material — to design elegant ceilings, cornices, rosettes, and more.

We transform homes and workspaces into stylish and personalized environments, tailored to each client’s taste and budget.

Our mission is to make artistic, high-end interior design accessible and affordable.
